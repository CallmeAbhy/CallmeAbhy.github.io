Following are the benefits for the life members:

Job Posting and referral: Only a life member can post a job/internship on the web portal.
Once the job gets posted it will be verified by the admin and then only the jobs will become live on the portal.
Only Life Member can ask for referrals.

Mentoring: They would be able to engage in mentoring activities.

They can engage with TISS/TISSAA as partner/collaborator/supporter in any projects/programs/training/studies.

Networking: They have a complete access to the alumni directory where they can view all the alumni on a world map.
Locate them on it and view their profiles.

Sharing Success Stories: The life member can share their lifetime experience, knowledge, skills on the portal.

They can upload the photos to the portal and can view the gallery.

They can view all their class members who have joint the portal.

They will be called for giving lectures/talk of their interest and expertise, share their experiences, can as students for internship and placements.

For Visitors

1. They will be able to make donations/contributions on the portal.

2. They will be able to view all the information which is public i.e. all our informational Opening text.
